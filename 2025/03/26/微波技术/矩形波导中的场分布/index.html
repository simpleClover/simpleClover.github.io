矩形波导中的场分布

由导行波分析可知矩形波导中不可能存在TEM波，只可能存在TM波和TE波。
对于TM波，波导内的电磁场由$E_z$确定。对于TE波，波导内的电磁场由$H_z$确定。下面我们将对于一个宽为a，高为b的矩形波导进行分析

矩形波导中的TM波

根据电磁波纵向分量满足的波动方程和边界条件，我们便可以算出波导内波的分布

$$ \left(\dfrac{\partial^{2}}{\partial x^2}+\dfrac{\partial^{2}}{\partial y^2}+\dfrac{\partial^{2}}{\partial z^2}\right)E_z+k^2E_z=0 $$

将$E_z(x,y,z)=E_z(x,y)e^{-\gamma z}$代入上式便可得到

$$ \left(\dfrac{\partial^{2}}{\partial x^2}+\dfrac{\partial^{2}}{\partial y^2}+k_c^2\right)E_z(x,y)=0 $$

此时分离变量，令$E_z(x,y)=f(x)g(y)$，$k_c^2=k_x^2+k_y^2$，则有

$$ \begin{array}{c} f''(x)g(y)+f(x)g''(y)+(k_x^2+k_y^2)f(x)g(y)=0 \\ \dfrac{f''(x)}{f(x)}+ k_x^2+\dfrac{g''(y)}{g(y)} +k_y^2 = 0 \end{array} $$

对于电场，其边界条件为

$$ \begin{array}{cc} E_z\big|_{x=0}=0 & E_z\big|_{x=a}=0 \\ E_z\big|_{y=0}=0 & E_z\big|_{y=b}=0 \end{array} $$

则问题可分为两个微分方程的求解

$$ \begin{array}{l} \begin{cases} f''(x)+k_x^2f(x)=0 \\ f(0)=0, f(a)=0 \end{cases} \\ \\ \begin{cases} g''(y)+k_y^2g(y)=0 \\ g(0)=0, g(b)=0 \end{cases} \end{array} $$

求其通解

$$ \begin{array}{c} f(x)=A\textrm{sin}(k_xx)+B\textrm{cos}(k_xx) \\ g(y)=C\textrm{sin}(k_yy)+D\textrm{cos}(k_yy) \end{array} $$

由$f(0)=0,g(0)=0$可知$B=0,D=0$。

再由$f(a)=0,g(b)=0$可得

$$ \begin{array}{l} f(x)=A\textrm{sin}(k_xa)=0\\ g(y)=C\textrm{sin}(k_yb)=0 \end{array} $$

即

$$ \begin{array}{lc} \displaystyle k_x=\frac{m\pi}{a} & m=1,2,3\dots \\ \displaystyle k_y=\frac{n\pi}{b} & n=1,2,3\dots \end{array} $$

将其带入分离变量的方程，则有

$$ \begin{array}{c} E_z(x,y)=f(x)g(y)=E_m\textrm{sin}(\dfrac{m\pi}{a}x)\textrm{sin}(\dfrac{n\pi}{b}y) \quad m,n=1,2,3\dots \\ k_{cmn}^2 = \left(\dfrac{m\pi}{a}\right)^2+\left(\dfrac{n\pi}{b}\right)^2 \end{array} $$

可见截止波数只与波导的尺寸有关，且一组$mn$对应一组模式，记作$\textrm{TM}_{mn}$

矩形波导中的TE波

与TM波分析方法类似，对于$H_z(x,y)=f(x)g(y)$，有

$$ \begin{array}{c} f(a)=A\textrm{sin}(k_xx)+B\textrm{cos}(k_xx) \\ g(bb)=C\textrm{sin}(k_yy)+D\textrm{cos}(k_yy) \end{array} $$

但与之不同的是边界条件，对于磁场其边界条件为

$$ \begin{array}{cc} \dfrac{\partial H_z}{\partial x}\big|_{x=0}=0 & \dfrac{\partial H_z}{\partial x}\big|_{x=a}=0 \\ \dfrac{\partial H_z}{\partial y}\big|_{y=0}=0 & \dfrac{\partial H_z}{\partial y}\big|_{y=b}=0 \end{array} $$

则有 $A=0,C=0$，且

$$ \begin{array}{c} f'(a) = -Bk_x\textrm{sin}(k_xa) = 0\\ g'(b) = -Dk_y\textrm{sin}(k_yb) = 0 \end{array} $$

则有

$$ \begin{array}{lc} \displaystyle k_x=\frac{m\pi}{a} & m=0,1,2\dots \\ \displaystyle k_y=\frac{n\pi}{b} & n=0,1,2\dots \end{array} $$

最终得到

$$ \begin{array}{c} H_z(x,y)=f(x)g(y)=H_m\textrm{cos}(\dfrac{m\pi}{a}x)\textrm{cos}(\dfrac{n\pi}{b}y) \quad m,n=0,1,2\dots \\ k_{cmn}^2 = \left(\dfrac{m\pi}{a}\right)^2+\left(\dfrac{n\pi}{b}\right)^2 \end{array} $$

注意到对于TM波m和n不可为0，但在TE波中m和n不能同时取0
